Fully Customized Programs

Our curriculum and exclusive experiences are tailored to fit your organization's size, schedules, and learning objectives. From a full day program for rising managers to a multi-day executive retreat, the George Washington Leadership Institute can meet your company's needs.

Program Highlights

Elevate Your Experience

The iconic Mount Vernon Estate offers participants a full range of unique and unforgettable opportunities. Participants can enjoy receptions on the mansion piazza, private candlelight tours, catered dinners throughout the historic grounds, specialized tours, and enlightening interactions with nationally-recognized historians. All dining, lodging, and transportation arrangements can be made in advance by the George Washington Leadership Institute staff.

Historic Excursion Programs

The George Washington Leadership Institute offers additional programming that will take you to historic sites throughout the mid-Atlantic. These immersive programs are led by true military historians who focus on the compelling actions, stories, and personalities that have made these places significant leadership lessons from American history. These programs can run for one day or multiple days depending on the organization's objectives and schedules.

Lessons in Leadership Series

The George Washington Leadership Institute's "Lessons in Leadership Series" provides engaging case studies that immerse readers in the actual problems that Washington faced, and insight into the decisions that started a nation. The entire series is available for download in iBook format for use on the iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

The George Washington Leadership Experience

The George Washington Leadership Experience is a program exclusively designed for college students and provides an exciting opportunity to examine the character and leadership of our nation's first president, George Washington.
